Institute for Muslim Affairs in Myanmar

We are an independent and non-governmental scholarly think tank to promote the issues of Muslim minority rights, highlighting minority participation in the political process of Myanmar with unique strategies and initiatives. We will follow liberal democratic values and foster the development of multiculturalism in the diverse communities of Burma.

Building Multicultural Society in Burma

Minority Matters

Our Missions

  • ○ Building federal democratic Burma
  • ○ Minority participation in the political arena of transitional and future Burma
  • ○ Development of multiculturalism between diverse communities of Burma
